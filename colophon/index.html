Colophon

This site is entirely handmade — HTML, CSS, and a tiny bit of Javascript. It will respect your device’s appearance and motion preferences.

It’s hosted by Netlify and deploys from Github.

Fonts are by Alanna Munro. Icons are from the Phosphor family.

I don’t use any trackers or analytics.

I continually work to make sure this site is accessible to everyone. If you catch something that needs improvement or isn’t working for you, please feel free to let me know.

But why?

I want a home online free from algorithms, corporate control, and bullshit AI chatbots; a place to express myself both personally and professionally, in that order. Really, I want to try to recapture a bit of the magic of my earliest days online.

Hyper-capitalism and rampant consumer culture are ugly, but the web — made by and for humans — can still be beautiful.
