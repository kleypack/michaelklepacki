Tracking the changes I make to this here website. Extremely incomplete.

2026

v 2026.1

・ 03 March 2026

Added a gaming log! Tracks most single-player campaigns I’ve completed going back to ~mid-2017. I’ve really loved tracking my reading so this was a natural next step, and it finally gives me something to do with all the shots I’ve taken in photo mode.

Reading has been moved to live with gaming under the new Media Log item on the home page.

Also — added this here changelog! Eventually I will go back and add earlier designs (maybe along with screenshots??) but that could be a slow process.

v 2026.0

・ 17 February 2026

Swapped out the upper navigation bar for a path bar, since the nav bar only held a fraction of the pages anyway. Makes it easy to find your way back home (with all available links) while leaving a nice trail of where you’ve been.

Speaking of links on the home page, their icons have gotten an update — colour! They also appear within the path bar. My site has been monochromatic for so long and I’m not sure how I feel about this change lol.

Speaking of colours, they’ve all been slightly updated. P3, baby! Increased contrast of secondary text.

Updated button styling to match the new path bar.

v 2025.1

・ 21 January 2026

Finally got around to uploading photos from the second half of 2025 and (hopefully) made the timelime more performant.

Big ol’ home page update! I liked the simplicity of my navigation bar (Home, Design, Hobbies, All), but it meant that so much of my site was buried away; so I added links to all pages right on the home page. Completely restructured my portfolio presentation. Instead of one, long Design page, I created a Portfolio page with sub-sections and individual pages for UI, icons, and exploration.

Moved “abbreviated history” from index to /portfolio. Moved “about this site” to its own /colophon page.
